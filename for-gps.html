What to Include in Your Referral

  • Patient demographics and contact details
  • Presenting complaint and relevant orthopaedic history
  • Relevant comorbidities and medications (especially anticoagulants, immunosuppressants)
  • Current imaging — please attach or state availability (X-rays ideally weight-bearing; MRI for soft tissue pathology)
  • Functional impact and patient goals
  • Any previous orthopaedic history or surgeries
  • Urgency of review

Referral Indications

The following guidance is provided to assist with appropriate patient selection. Please call our rooms if you have any questions about whether a patient is suitable for review.

Hip Replacement Indications

Consider referring patients with:

  • Symptomatic hip osteoarthritis failing conservative management (physio, analgesia, activity modification, injections)
  • Avascular necrosis of the femoral head
  • Inflammatory arthropathy with significant hip involvement
  • Post-traumatic hip arthritis
  • Failed previous hip surgery requiring revision

Knee Replacement Indications

Consider referring patients with:

  • Advanced tricompartmental or unicompartmental knee osteoarthritis
  • Inflammatory arthropathy with significant knee involvement
  • Failed conservative management
  • Significant functional limitation impacting quality of life
  • Varus/valgus deformity with isolated compartment disease (partial replacement candidate)

Sports Knee Surgery Indications

Consider referring patients with:

  • Complete ACL rupture confirmed on MRI (acute or chronic in active patients)
  • Symptomatic meniscal tear not responding to conservative treatment
  • Mechanical symptoms (locking, giving way, significant effusions)
  • Patella instability
  • Cartilage lesions requiring surgical assessment

Post-operative Care for GPs

Following surgery with Mr Bairstow, your ongoing involvement in the patient's care is valued. GP management of post-operative wound care, medication review, and comorbidity management contributes significantly to a safe and successful recovery.

Post-operative protocols and wound care guidelines are available from our rooms — please contact reception on (08) 9271 1552 or email drbrettbairstow@westnet.com.au.
